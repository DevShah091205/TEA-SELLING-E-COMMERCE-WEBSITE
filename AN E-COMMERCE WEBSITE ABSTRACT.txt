Abstract

The project “Heritage and Aroma – Tea Selling E-Commerce Website” is an online platform that enables customers to explore and purchase a variety of premium teas. It offers features like product browsing, secure payments, order tracking, and customer feedback.


 The admin panel allows easy management of products, orders, and inventory. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the website provides a responsive and user-friendly interface, promoting the rich tradition of tea through modern digital technology.
